\section{DifferentialRiccatiEquations.jl}

This section describes the general properties of the \julia{DifferentialRiccatiEquations.jl} package.

\begin{itemize}
  \item
    Type of compression (none, $LDL^\T$) is selected by dispatching on the type of $Q$.
    For a dense matrix \julia{Q::Matrix}, a dense solver will be used.
    For a \ac{LRSIF} \julia{Q::\LDLt}, a low-rank solver will be used.
  \item
    \julia{Q::\LDLt} of rank $n_Q$ consisting of $L_Q \in\R^{n\times n_Q}$ and $D_Q\in\R^{n_Q\times n_Q}$ will be compressed,
    \cf~\autoref{sec:lr:compression},
    if the inner dimension becomes too big, $n_Q \geq 0.5 n$,
    and every 20 modifications without compression,
    \cf~\autoref{sec:lr:update}.
    For now, this is not configurable.
\end{itemize}
